About Me

Hello! My name is Luca Lopriore, and I am a photographer and filmmaker with a passion for capturing life's moments. Whether it's through sports photography, event coverage, or creating compelling short films, I strive to tell unique and meaningful stories.

I began my creative journey by exploring local events and collaborating with communities to document their stories. Over time, I’ve honed my skills in editing, directing, and photography, constantly pushing myself to learn and grow.

When I’m not behind the camera, I enjoy exploring new places, watching classic films, and experimenting with different visual styles to bring fresh perspectives to my work. Let’s create something amazing together!
